🔍 API ENDPOINTS DISCOVERED

Base: https://android.lonoapp.net/api

GET /books
  Parameters: limit, page, sex, status, kind
  Response: List of books with metadata

GET /books/{id}
  Response: Detailed book information

GET /chapters?book_id={id}
  Parameters: book_id, page, limit
  Response: List of chapters

GET /chapters/{id}
  Response: Chapter content
